## ðŸ”„ Skill Process

### Step 1: Determine Post Type

Based on the day and strategy:
- **Monday:** Industry insights and trends
- **Tuesday:** Tips and best practices
- **Wednesday:** Case study or client success
- **Thursday:** Problem-solving content
- **Friday:** Weekly recap or personal story

### Step 2: Generate Post Content

**Content Structure:**
1. **Hook (First Line):** Grab attention immediately
   - Use emoji for visual appeal
   - Ask a question or make a bold statement
   - Create curiosity

2. **Body (3-5 paragraphs):**
   - Provide value (insight, tip, story)
   - Use short paragraphs (1-2 lines each)
   - Include bullet points or numbered lists
   - Add white space for readability

3. **Call-to-Action:**
   - Ask for engagement (comment, share, DM)
   - Provide clear next step
   - Make it easy to respond

4. **Hashtags (3-5):**
   - Mix popular and niche hashtags
   - Relevant to content and industry
   - Include branded hashtag if applicable

### Step 3: Optimize for Engagement

**Best Practices:**
- Keep it under 1,300 characters (ideal: 800-1,000)
- Use emojis strategically (2-4 per post)
- Include line breaks for readability
- Ask questions to encourage comments
- Tag relevant people/companies (when appropriate)
- Post at optimal times: 8 AM, 12 PM, or 5 PM

**Engagement Triggers:**
- Controversial (but professional) opinions
- Personal stories with lessons
- Data and statistics
- "How to" content
- Behind-the-scenes insights

### Step 4: Create Approval Request

Generate approval file with:
- Full post content
- Rationale for the post
- Expected reach and engagement
- Optimal posting time
- Expiry time (24 hours)

### Step 5: Track Performance

After posting:
- Monitor engagement (likes, comments, shares)
- Track profile views
- Note connection requests
- Identify high-performing content types
- Adjust strategy based on data
