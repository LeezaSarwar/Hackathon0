## ğŸ”„ Skill Process

### Step 1: Determine if Approval Required

**Always Require Approval For:**
- Sending emails to new contacts
- LinkedIn posts (all)
- WhatsApp messages to new leads
- Financial commitments over $50
- Contract or pricing discussions
- Refunds or compensation
- Sensitive topics (complaints, legal, HR)
- System configuration changes
- Data deletion or modification
- External API calls (first time)

**No Approval Needed For:**
- Reading and analyzing files
- Creating task summaries
- Updating dashboard
- Organizing files
- Creating plans (not executing)
- Routine responses to existing clients
- Information gathering

### Step 2: Assess Risk Level

**Risk Levels:**

**HIGH RISK:**
- Financial impact > $100
- Legal implications
- Reputation risk
- Irreversible actions
- New client relationships
- Expiry: 4 hours

**MEDIUM RISK:**
- Financial impact $50-$100
- Moderate reputation impact
- Reversible with effort
- Existing client communications
- Expiry: 24 hours

**LOW RISK:**
- Financial impact < $50
- Minimal reputation impact
- Easily reversible
- Routine operations
- Expiry: 48 hours

### Step 4: Set Expiry Time

**Expiry Guidelines:**
- **Critical (P0):** 2 hours
- **High (P1):** 4 hours
- **Medium (P2):** 24 hours
- **Low (P3):** 48 hours

**After Hours Adjustment:**
- If created after 6 PM, extend expiry to next business day 10 AM
- If created on Friday evening, extend to Monday 10 AM
- Urgent items: No adjustment, but send notification
